Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Brown beef and Italian sausage in a large pan. Drain of fat. With the Italian sausage, I usually press on it with a paper towel too.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles in water, dash salt, and bit of oil. Lay out on towels after done.
  3. Cover bottom of pan with thin layer of meat mixture (meats, spaghetti sauce, spices).
  4. Cover with one layer of noodles.
  5. Cover with one layer of noodles.
